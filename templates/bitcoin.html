Bitcoin resources



Here you will find educational content about Bitcoin. It's divided into four sections : the basics of Bitcoin, Bitcoin & money, Bitcoin & investing, and more.

I hope you'll find the articles interesting, and, more importantly, that you will understand Bitcoin a little bit better after reading them!


You can start here with the Bitcoin whitepaper from Satoshi Nakamoto.


Bitcoin as a technological innovation


  • Explain Bitcoin like I'm five

    With a very concrete example using an apple, the author gives a simple and intuitive explanation of how bitcoin is a true technological innovation.



  • Why Bitcoin is different

    This article explains how the true innovation behind Bitcoin is the decentralized digital scarcity, and how important that is.



  • Edward Snowden's explanation to his lawyer

    This is a conversation between Edward Snowden and his lawyer, where Snowden explains with practical examples what bitcoin and blockchain are and what makes them special.



  • How Bitcoin works

    After seeing that Bitcoin is a true innovation, this short video (5 min) explains how bitcoin works from a technical perspective. Don't worry, there is nothing here you shouldn't be able to understand.





Bitcoin as a new form of money


In his series of articles "The Bullish Case for Bitcoin", Vijay Boyapati compares Bitcoin to gold and fiat currencies, and talks about its advantages as a new form of money. Descriptions of Part 2, 3, and 4 are his own.


  • The Origin of Money

    In the first part, the author goes back to the origin of money and look at how valuable it is to have the best possible store of value and mean of exchange.



  • Attributes of a Store of Value : Bitcoin versus Gold and Fiat

    In part 2, I cover the attributes that make a good store of value and how Bitcoin compares to other monetary goods, such as gold and fiat currencies.



  • The Bullish Case for Bitcoin

    In part 3, I cover the evolution of money, how a monetary good transitions from a store of value to a medium of exchange and where Bitcoin is in its evolution to a fully fledged global money.



  • The Bullish Case for Bitcoin

    In part 4, I will tackle some of the major arguments against Bitcoin and why they are incorrect when understood in terms of the economic framework that has been discussed in parts 1, 2 and 3 of this article. I will also consider some of the genuine risks that face Bitcoin on its path toward full monetization.
